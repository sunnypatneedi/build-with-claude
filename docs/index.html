Claudachi

A kid-safe virtual pet for the Cardputer-Adv. Install in two clicks.

1

Install firmware

Plug the Cardputer-Adv into your computer with a USB-C data cable. If nothing happens when you click, try flipping the cable 180° — Cardputer-Adv only enumerates one orientation.

Your browser doesn't support WebSerial. Use Chrome, Edge, or Brave on a desktop. WebSerial is only available over HTTPS.
What does this do?

Writes M5Stack's UIFlow 2.0 firmware (~8 MB) to the device. When prompted, you'll need to do the button dance on the back of the device:

  1. Hold G0
  2. Tap RST (still holding G0)
  3. Release RST
  4. Release G0 after ~1 sec — screen goes dark

The buttons are flush-mounted on the back of the device; a fingernail helps.

2

Install apps

Once the firmware finishes and the device reboots, click below to push the Claudachi app (and the rest of the buddy bundle) onto the device. The browser will ask you to pick the serial port again.

What does this do?

Sends ~150 KB of MicroPython files into /flash/ on the device via the REPL: the Claudachi pet, the Snake game, the Hello demo, the Claude Buddy BLE client, plus their shared modules. Nothing leaves your machine.

3

Try Claudachi

Power-cycle the device with the side switch. The launcher menu shows up — pick claudachi and meet your new friend. Keys: F feed · P play · Z nap · T talk · Q back.
